# Gemini 3.5 Flash















Gemini 3.5 Flash provides sustained frontier-level intelligence optimized for
real-world tasks at a higher speed and lower cost. Designed for the agentic era,
it excels at sub-agent deployment, multi-step workflows, and long-horizon tasks
at scale. This model is particularly effective for rapid agentic loops involving
complex coding cycles and iterations.

 [Try in Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/prompts/new_chat?model=gemini-3.5-flash)
