# Create an Account

> Sign up for Vaani, set up your workspace, and get oriented in the dashboard

Your Vaani account is the hub for everything — agents, phone numbers, API keys, and call history.

## Sign Up

1. Go to app.vaanivoice.ai and click **Sign Up**. You can register with your email address.
2. Verify Your Email — Check your inbox for a verification email from Vaani. Click the confirmation link to activate your account.
3. Set Up Your Workspace — After verifying, you'll be prompted to name your workspace. A **workspace** is your team's shared environment — all agents, API keys, phone numbers, and call data live here.

## Your Dashboard

Once you're logged in, you'll land on your dashboard:

* **Agent Config** — where you'll create and manage your voice agents
* **Call History** — where completed calls, transcripts, and recordings appear
* **Settings** — where you'll find Telephony setup (phone numbers) and API key management
